## Notes

- **tʃ**, **ɟ**, **ŋ** and **ʔ** are consonants
- **ɨ** is a vowel; **au** is a diphthong (one vowel)
- **ˈ** indicates stressed syllable
- **¹ ² ³ ⁴ ⁵** denote tones (low 1 < 2 < 3 < 4 < 5 high; ⁴³ = falling)
- Tilde below vowel (a̰) = creaky voice
- Tilde above vowel (ã) = nasal sound
